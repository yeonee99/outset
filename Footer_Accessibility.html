Accessibility

  • THE OUTSET ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT

    At The Outset, we strive to provide a positive customer experience to all our customers, and we aim to promote accessibility and inclusion. Our goal is to permit all our customers to successfully gather information and transact business through our website and make your use of The Outset’s website a successful and enjoyable experience.

  • ACTIONS

    We are taking a variety of steps and devoting resources to further enhance the accessibility of our website. As part of these efforts, The Outset is committed to digital accessibility and works with experienced accessibility consultant, Accessible360, to optimize the on-site experience based on the WCAG 2.1 guideline for accessibility at Levels A and AA.

  • ACCESSIBILITY ASSISTANCE

    If you have difficulty using or accessing any element of The Outset’s website email us at help@theoutset.com and we will work with you to provide the information, item, or transaction you seek through a communication method that is accessible for you consistent with applicable law.

  • OUR ONGOING EFFORTS

    We at The Outset are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone, and applying the relevant accessibility standards. Please contact us at help@theoutset.com if you have any feedback or suggestions as to how we could improve the accessibility of this website.
