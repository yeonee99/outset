•The Cleanser
Wet skin with warm water and massage a dime-size amount of cleanser gently into skin, creating a light lather. Rinse thoroughly, and pat dry. Use daily, morning and night.
•The Prep Serum
After cleansing, apply a few pumps of serum to the skin in upwards strokes. Follow with your moisturizer. Use daily, morning and night.
•The Daily Moisturizer
after cleansing and prepping with a few drops of serum, smooth moisturizer gently into the face and neck.
•The Boosting Oil
For your skin, apply two to three drops on clean, dry skin, smoothing over the face and neck until absorbed. Can be used alone or mixed into a moisturizer for optimal hydration. Use daily each morning and night.
For your nails, add one or two drops to your cuticles to instantly soften and strengthen.
For your hair, apply a few drops to ends of clean, dry hair to add extra shine.
•The Eye Cream
Using your forefinger, gently tap a pea sized amount to the eye area and wherever needed to reduce the appearance of fine lines. Use daily, morning and night.
•The Micro Polish
Gently massage a small amount into slightly damp skin using circular motions for 1 minute. Avoid the eye and lip area. Rinse thoroughly.
•The Night Cream
After cleansing, apply directly to the face and neck in gentle upwards strokes before bed. Use nightly. The Eye Cream
• Cleansing Micelles(The Cleanser) : atracts and dissolves makeup, oil and other impurities without drying the skin. • Vegan Collagen(The Prep Serum) : this clean alternative to traditional collagen promotes firmer-looking skin. • Squalane(The Daily Moisturizer) : sourced from olives, this emollient mimics the skin's natural oils to leave skin feeling smooth and moisturized. • Fine Perlite(The Micro Polish) : a mineral that is naturally spherical in shape to provide gentle physical exfoliation without abrading the skin. • Niacinamide(The Night Cream) : helps to brighten and improve skin texture • Ascorbyl Glucoside(The Eye Cream) : a stable form of Vitamin C that brightens and revitalizes the skin. • Abyssinian Oil(The Boosting Oil) : Rich in omega-9 and omega-6 fatty acids, this deeply moisturizing oil helps to cushion and comfort dry, sensitive skin.
