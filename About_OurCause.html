OUR CAUSES

FOSTERING NEW BEGINNINGS

The Outset aims to foster new beginnings for people and the planet - an ethos woven through the brand from product to mission. Our approach is simple - consistent acts of kindness via advocacy, fundraising, and volunteerism that will make a meaningful impact. By supporting organizations that help empower people and protect the environment, our goal is to help write the next chapter, look to the future with optimism, and demonstrate that any day can be The Start of You™.

  • MEET OUR PARTNERS DRESS FOR SUCCESS

    When you help one woman, you also help her family and an entire community. As Global Ambassador for Dress for Success (DFS), Scarlett will bring attention to the organization’s crucial mission to end gender-based inequality and cycles of despair. As a brand, we are committed to supporting DFS programs that help women as they return to the labor force. Dress for Success (DFS) is the leading global non-profit employment resource for women helping them find jobs, advance their careers, and become economically independent. What began in a church basement in New York City has now become a global movement.

    LEARN MORE ABOUT DRESS FOR SUCCESS

  • MEET OUR PARTNERS ONE TREE PLANTED

    A non-profit focused on global reforestation, One Tree Planted aims to restore forests, create habitat for biodiversity and make a positive social impact around the world. For every dollar donated, a tree is planted. Trees play a vital role in creating clean air and water, a healthy climate, and providing habitats for wildlife. That’s why we are committing to U.S. Planting Projects that help to build back forests destroyed by wildfires and Urban Forestry Projects that will make a difference in local communities. We encourage you to join us in our reforestation efforts.

    LEARN MORE ABOUT ONE TREE PLANTED

  • MEET OUR PARTNERS 1% FOR THE PLANET

    We are proud to be a member of 1% for the Planet, a global organization that exists to ensure our planet and future generations thrive. From the beginning, we have pledged to donate 1% of annual sales to support nonprofit organizations focused on the environment, like One Tree Planted.

    LEARN MORE ABOUT 1% FOR THE PLANET
