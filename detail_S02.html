  • WHAT SKIN TYPE IS BEST SUITED TO USE THIS SERUM?

    This everyday serum is suited for all skin types, including sensitive skin. This formula is also non-comedogenic, so it won't clog your pores.

  • HOW DOES THIS SERUM FIT INTO MY SKINCARE ROUTINE?

    Our fortifying serum can easily incorporated to anyone's routine to help plump and firm the skin. It layers well under moisturizer and leaves skin looking smoother and brighter.

  • WHAT OTHER PRODUCTS PAIR WELL WITH THIS ONE?

    For best results, use this prep serum in combination with the Gentle Micellar Antioxidant Cleanser and Nourishing Squalane Daily Moisturizer in the Daily Essentials Regimen. These three steps are all you need for healthy, hydrated and balanced skin.
